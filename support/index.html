Frequently Asked Questions

What is WeDrink?

WeDrink is a cocktail discovery app that helps you save your home bar ingredients, find recipes you can make, discover new drinks, and collect favorites.

How do recommendations work?

Recommendations are based on saved ingredients, saved recipes, and usage activity inside the app.

Can I use WeDrink if I do not drink alcohol?

WeDrink is intended for users who have reached the legal drinking age in their location. Please follow local laws and use the app responsibly.
