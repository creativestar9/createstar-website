Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Creative Star collects, uses, and protects information when you use WeDrink.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address
  • Login information
  • Saved ingredients
  • Saved recipes
  • Usage data, such as app interactions and feature activity

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide account login and authentication
  • Recommend cocktail recipes based on saved ingredients
  • Save ingredients, recipes, and collection preferences
  • Improve product quality, reliability, and user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We may use trusted service providers to operate app features, store data, analyze usage, or provide account services. These providers are only allowed to process information for the services they provide to us.

Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To request deletion, contact us at support@YOUR_DOMAIN.com.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children

WeDrink is intended for users who have reached the legal drinking age in their location. We do not knowingly collect information from users who are not legally allowed to consume alcohol.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
